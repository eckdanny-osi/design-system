## Synopsis

`Tooltip`s provide a supplemental description or explanation of an object or control. Users may trigger the appearance of a tool tip by hovering over an element.

## Usage Guidelines

Use tooltips for elements that regularly and repeatedly appear throughout the application, but may nevertheless require clarification. Unlike an infotip, the presence of a tooltip is not graphically indicated.

### Content Guidelines

Tooltips should be used for short terminology definition.
