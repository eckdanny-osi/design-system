## Synopsis

The components: `Container`, `Row`, and `Col` are used to structure content into a grid.

<DemoCard>
  <div>
    <Row>
      <Col sm="6">
        <Card className="p-2">Col</Card>
      </Col>
      <Col sm="6">
        <Card className="p-2">Col</Card>
      </Col>
    </Row>
    <Row>
      <Col sm="3">
        <Card className="p-2 mb-0">Col</Card>
      </Col>
      <Col sm="6">
        <Card className="p-2 mb-0">Col</Card>
      </Col>
      <Col sm="3">
        <Card className="p-2 mb-0">Col</Card>
      </Col>
    </Row>
  </div>
</DemoCard>
