## Cards

Some `Card` variants and experimental behaviors.

### Structure

<Card>
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle>Card Title</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>
  <CardBody>CardBody</CardBody>
  <CardFooter>CardFooter</CardFooter>
</Card>
